Key Points to Mention

Boundary Waters

The Boundary Waters Canoe Area Wilderness is a national treasure that should be protected from mining interests. Sulfide-ore copper mining poses significant risks to this pristine ecosystem.

National Forests

Minnesota's national forests belong to all Americans. Sustainable forest management should prioritize conservation and recreation, not just logging interests.

Medicaid & Healthcare

Medicaid provides essential healthcare for vulnerable Minnesotans. Cutting or privatizing these services harms our communities and increases costs for everyone.

Social Security

Social Security is a promise to Americans who have paid into the system their entire working lives. It should be strengthened, not undermined.
